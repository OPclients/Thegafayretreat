Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when visitors contact us, request availability, make a booking enquiry, or communicate with us through the website, WhatsApp, phone, email, or social media.

Information we collect

When you contact us or send a booking enquiry, we may collect the information you choose to provide, including your full name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, preferred date, number of guests or travelers, selected service, pickup location, hotel or riad details, destination preferences, dietary requests, special requests, and message content.

Technical information

Our website may collect limited technical information needed for normal operation, performance, security, and user experience. This may include browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location, referral source, and interaction data if analytics or similar tools are active.

How we use your information

We use personal information to reply to enquiries, check availability, prepare quotes, confirm bookings, arrange pickup or transfers, coordinate services, communicate before and during the experience, improve our services, prevent misuse, and keep necessary business records.

Communication channels

We may communicate with guests through WhatsApp, phone, email, social media, or external travel platforms. When you choose to use these channels, your information may also be processed according to the privacy policies of those external services.

Sharing personal information

We do not sell personal information. We may share only the necessary booking details with trusted service providers when required to deliver the requested service, such as drivers, guides, hotels, camps, restaurants, activity providers, transport partners, payment providers, or booking platforms.

Payments

If payments or deposits are arranged, payment information may be processed by banks, payment providers, booking platforms, or other secure payment services. We do not request or store full card details through unsecured channels such as normal messages or email.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, provide services, handle disputes, comply with legal or accounting requirements, and improve customer service. When information is no longer needed, it should be deleted, anonymized, or securely archived.

Cookies and external tools

The website may use essential technical features and may include links or embedded tools from external services such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, analytics tools, booking platforms, or payment services. These services may use their own cookies or tracking technologies. Visitors can manage cookies through their browser settings.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no website, email, messaging app, or online transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. Visitors should avoid sending sensitive identity or payment information through unsecured channels.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, visitors may request access to their personal information, ask for correction, request deletion, object to certain processing, or withdraw consent where applicable. To make a request, visitors can contact us through the details listed on the Contact page.

Children and family bookings

Our services may be suitable for families, but booking requests should be made by adults. If information about children is provided as part of a family booking, it will only be used to organize the requested service.

External links

Our website may link to external websites and apps such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, payment providers, accommodation providers, or booking platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of these third-party services.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, website, legal requirements, or business practices. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or personal data requests, please contact us through the contact details listed on the Contact page.
