Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Agafay Retreat collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when visitors contact us, request availability, make a booking enquiry, communicate with us, or use our website.

Information we collect

When visitors contact us or send a booking enquiry, we may collect information such as full name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, preferred date, number of guests, selected experience, pickup location, accommodation details needed for transfer, dietary requests, special requests, and message content. If visitors interact with external platforms linked from our website, those platforms may collect information according to their own policies.

Technical information

Our website may collect limited technical information needed for normal operation, security, performance, and user experience. This may include device type, browser type, pages visited, approximate location, referral source, and interaction data if analytics or similar tools are active.

How we use personal information

We use personal information to reply to enquiries, check availability, prepare quotes, confirm bookings, organize transfers, arrange activities, manage meals and dietary requests, prepare private setups, communicate with guests, improve our services, prevent misuse, and keep necessary business records.

Communication channels

The Agafay Retreat may communicate with guests through WhatsApp, phone, email, social media, Tripadvisor, or other booking platforms. When guests choose to use these channels, their information may also be processed according to the privacy policies of those external services.

Legal basis

We process personal information because it is necessary to respond to enquiries, take steps before confirming a booking, provide requested services, manage customer communication, meet legal or accounting obligations, protect legitimate business interests, and, where required, based on consent.

Sharing personal information

We do not sell personal information. We may share only necessary booking details with trusted service providers when required to deliver the requested service, such as drivers, guides, activity partners, dining teams, accommodation partners, event suppliers, payment providers, or booking platforms. We share only the information needed for the service.

Payments

If payments or deposits are arranged, payment information may be processed by banks, payment providers, booking platforms, or other secure payment services. The Agafay Retreat should not request or store full card details through unsecured channels such as normal messages or email.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, provide services, handle disputes, comply with legal or accounting requirements, and improve customer service. When information is no longer needed, it should be deleted, anonymized, or securely archived.

Cookies and external tools

The website may use essential technical features and may include links or embedded tools from external services such as Google Maps, Tripadvisor, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, analytics tools, or booking/payment platforms. These services may use their own cookies or tracking technologies. Visitors can manage cookies through their browser settings.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no website, email, messaging app, or online transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. Visitors should avoid sending sensitive identity or payment information through unsecured channels.

International visitors

The Agafay Retreat may receive enquiries from visitors outside Morocco. By contacting us or requesting a booking, visitors understand that their information may be processed in Morocco and through international communication platforms or service providers used to organize the experience.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, visitors may request access to their personal information, ask for correction, request deletion, object to certain processing, or withdraw consent where applicable. To make a request, visitors can contact us through the details listed on the Contact page.

Children

Our services may be suitable for families, but booking requests should be made by adults. If information about children is provided as part of a family booking, it will only be used to organize the requested experience.

External links

Our website may link to external websites and apps such as Tripadvisor, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Google Maps, payment providers, or booking platforms. The Agafay Retreat is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of these third-party services.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, website, legal requirements, or business practices. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or personal data requests, please contact The Agafay Retreat through the contact details listed on the Contact page.
