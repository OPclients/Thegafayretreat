Terms & Conditions

These Terms & Conditions explain the rules for booking, visiting, and enjoying experiences with The Agafay Retreat. By sending a booking request, confirming a reservation, making a payment, or visiting the retreat, guests agree to these terms.

Booking confirmation

A request sent through the website, WhatsApp, phone, email, social media, Tripadvisor, or another channel is not automatically confirmed. A booking is confirmed only when The Agafay Retreat confirms the date, time, experience details, number of guests, price, pickup details when applicable, and any required payment or deposit.

Prices and availability

Prices may vary depending on the season, date, number of guests, selected experience, transport needs, meal options, private setup, decoration, overnight stay type, and supplier availability. Prices shown or quoted are subject to availability until the booking is confirmed. Final pricing is always confirmed before payment or arrival.

Payments and deposits

Some experiences, overnight stays, private events, romantic setups, proposal setups, birthday arrangements, and group bookings may require a deposit or full payment in advance. Payment instructions will be shared before confirmation. A booking may be released if the required payment is not received by the agreed time.

Guest information

Guests are responsible for providing accurate information, including full name, contact number, number of guests, preferred date, pickup location, dietary needs, allergies, health concerns, mobility limitations, and any special requests. The Agafay Retreat is not responsible for issues caused by incorrect or incomplete information provided by guests.

Guest responsibilities

Guests must arrive on time, respect staff and guides, follow safety instructions, respect animals, avoid damaging property, respect other guests, and protect the desert environment. Aggressive, unsafe, disrespectful, or disruptive behavior may result in refusal of service or ending the experience without refund.

Activities and safety

Activities such as quad biking, camel riding, pool use, desert walks, transfers, and outdoor dining involve normal travel and outdoor activity risks. Guests must follow guide and staff instructions at all times. The Agafay Retreat may adjust, delay, or refuse an activity because of safety, weather, health, operational, or behavior concerns.

Quad biking and camel riding

Quad biking and camel riding may be operated by guides or activity partners. Guests must respect age, safety, clothing, and behavior requirements. Helmets or safety equipment must be used when required. Guests who ignore guide instructions, drive recklessly, or create danger may be refused participation without refund.

Transfers and pickup

Transfers from Marrakech depend on traffic, road conditions, pickup location, hotel or riad access, and driver availability. Some riads, medina streets, or pedestrian areas may require guests to meet at a nearby accessible pickup point. Pickup and return times are estimates and may vary.

Food, allergies, and dietary requests

Guests should inform The Agafay Retreat in advance about allergies, dietary needs, vegetarian requests, or special meal requirements. We do our best to accommodate requests, but availability may depend on the menu, suppliers, timing, and confirmed booking details. Guests with severe allergies should take appropriate precautions.

Weather and operational changes

Desert experiences may be affected by weather, wind, heat, rain, road conditions, safety issues, supplier availability, or operational needs. The Agafay Retreat may change timing, adjust the program, replace an activity, offer a date change, or propose another suitable solution when necessary.

Private setups and events

Private dinners, romantic setups, proposals, birthdays, group events, photography requests, music, decoration, and special arrangements require advance planning. Final details, timing, inclusions, price, payment terms, and cancellation conditions must be confirmed before the booking is considered final.

Photos and media

Guests may take personal photos and videos during their visit unless restricted for safety, privacy, or operational reasons. The Agafay Retreat will only use clearly identifiable guest photos or videos for marketing with guest permission. Guests who do not want to appear in any media should inform the team.

Limitation of liability

However, we are not responsible for delays, changes, losses, injuries, missed connections, or additional costs caused by circumstances outside our reasonable control, including weather, traffic, road conditions, guest behavior, third-party providers, force majeure, or government restrictions, except where required by law.

Contact
