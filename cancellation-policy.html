Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, rescheduling, no-shows, weather changes, transfers, and private setups are handled for experiences booked with The Agafay Retreat. By confirming a booking, guests agree to the cancellation terms below.

How to cancel or change a booking

To cancel or change a booking, guests must contact The Agafay Retreat as early as possible by WhatsApp or email. The request should include the guest name, booking date, selected experience, number of guests, and any confirmation details. Cancellation and change requests are considered received only when our team confirms receipt.

Standard experiences

For standard experiences such as camel rides, quad biking, pool days, sunset dinners, dinner shows, and regular day experiences, the following cancellation rules apply unless different terms were agreed in writing:

  • Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled start time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made between 24 and 48 hours before the scheduled start time are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

Overnight stays

For overnight stays, cancellations made 7 days or more before the check-in date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made between 3 and 7 days before check-in are eligible for a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 72 hours before check-in are non-refundable. Date changes may be offered when availability allows.

Private setups and events

Private dinners, romantic setups, proposals, birthdays, group events, custom decoration, and private celebrations require advance preparation and supplier coordination. For these bookings, cancellations made 7 days or more before the event are eligible for a full refund excluding any non-refundable supplier costs already paid. Cancellations made between 3 and 7 days before the event are eligible for a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 72 hours before the event are non-refundable.

Deposits

Some bookings may require a deposit to secure the date, staff, transport, accommodation, activities, meals, or private setup. Deposit refundability follows the cancellation rules for the booked experience. If suppliers or special materials have already been reserved or purchased for a private setup, those costs may be deducted from any eligible refund.

No-shows and late arrivals

Guests who do not arrive at the agreed pickup point or retreat location without contacting us may be considered a no-show. No-shows are non-refundable. Late arrivals may reduce the duration of the experience and may not qualify for a refund or extension, especially when guides, drivers, dining teams, or activity schedules are already arranged.

Transfers and pickup changes

Transfer arrangements depend on pickup location, road access, driver availability, traffic, and timing. If guests change the pickup location or pickup time after confirmation, additional fees may apply. If the driver has already started the journey, transfer fees may be non-refundable.

Weather and safety

Agafay desert experiences may be affected by wind, rain, heat, road conditions, or safety concerns. If The Agafay Retreat must adjust an experience for weather, safety, or operational reasons, we may offer a time change, date change, alternative experience, or refund depending on the situation. Guest safety always comes first.

External booking platforms

If a booking is made through an external platform such as Tripadvisor or another booking partner, the cancellation and refund terms shown on that platform may apply. Guests should check their booking confirmation and contact the platform when required.

Refund processing

Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method where possible. Processing times may vary depending on the bank, payment provider, or booking platform. Transfer fees, bank fees, platform fees, and currency conversion differences may not be refundable.

Contact

For cancellation, refund, or date-change requests, please contact The Agafay Retreat through the WhatsApp, phone, or email details listed on the Contact page.
