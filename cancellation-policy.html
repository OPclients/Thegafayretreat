Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, date changes, no-shows, weather issues, and supplier changes are handled. By confirming a booking, guests agree to the cancellation terms below.

How to cancel or change a booking

To cancel or change a booking, guests must contact us as early as possible by WhatsApp or email. The request should include the guest name, booking date, selected service, number of guests or travelers, and confirmation details. Cancellation and change requests are considered received only when our team confirms receipt.

Standard day experiences

For standard day experiences, day trips, local excursions, activities, dinners, pool days, and similar one-day services, the following rules apply unless different terms were agreed in writing:

  • Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled start time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made between 24 and 48 hours before the scheduled start time are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

Multi-day trips and overnight stays

For multi-day trips, desert tours, overnight stays, accommodation-based experiences, and extended itineraries, the following rules apply unless different terms were agreed in writing:

  • Cancellations made 7 days or more before departure or check-in are eligible for a full refund, excluding non-refundable supplier costs already paid.
  • Cancellations made between 3 and 7 days before departure or check-in are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 72 hours before departure or check-in are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

Private bookings and custom arrangements

Private tours, custom programs, special setups, private events, group bookings, special accommodation requests, and tailor-made itineraries require advance planning and supplier coordination. For these bookings, cancellations made 14 days or more before the date are eligible for a full refund excluding non-refundable supplier costs already paid. Cancellations made between 7 and 14 days before the date are eligible for a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 7 days before the date are non-refundable.

Deposits

Some bookings may require a deposit to secure drivers, guides, accommodation, activities, meals, staff, suppliers, or special arrangements. Deposit refundability follows the cancellation rules for the booked service. If suppliers or special services have already been reserved or paid for, those non-refundable costs may be deducted from any eligible refund.

No-shows and late arrivals

Guests who do not arrive at the agreed pickup point, departure location, or service location without contacting us may be considered a no-show. No-shows are non-refundable. Late arrivals may reduce the duration of the service and may not qualify for a refund or extension, especially when staff, drivers, guides, accommodation, activities, or dining arrangements are already scheduled.

Pickup changes

Pickup arrangements depend on location, road access, driver availability, traffic, and timing. If guests change the pickup location or pickup time after confirmation, additional fees may apply. If the driver has already started the journey, transfer or transport fees may be non-refundable.

Supplier costs

For bookings involving accommodation, camps, restaurants, guides, transport providers, decoration, private setups, or activity suppliers, some supplier costs may be non-refundable. Non-refundable supplier costs already paid on behalf of the guest may be deducted from any eligible refund.

Weather, safety, and operational changes

Experiences may be affected by weather, wind, rain, heat, road conditions, traffic, attraction closures, or safety concerns. If we must adjust a booking for weather, safety, or operational reasons, we may offer a time change, date change, alternative activity, travel credit, or refund depending on the situation.

Date changes and rescheduling

Date changes may be possible depending on availability, season, accommodation, drivers, guides, staff, and supplier conditions. Please request changes as early as possible. Price differences may apply if the new date, guest number, accommodation level, or service details change.

Refund processing

Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method where possible. Processing times may vary depending on the bank, payment provider, or booking platform. Transfer fees, bank fees, platform fees, and currency conversion differences may not be refundable.

External booking platforms

If a booking is made through an external platform or travel marketplace, the cancellation and refund terms shown on that platform may also apply. Guests should check their booking confirmation and contact the platform when required.

Contact

For cancellation, refund, or date-change requests, please contact us through the WhatsApp, phone, or email details listed on the Contact page.
